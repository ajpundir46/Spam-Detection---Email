TF-IDF Explanation
##################################
1. Term Frequency (TF):
TF = (Number of times a word appears in a document) / (Total words in the document)

Example:
Document: "I love machine learning machine"
"machine" appears 2 times out of 5 words
TF = 2/5 = 0.4


2. Inverse Document Frequency (IDF):
IDF = log(Total number of documents / Number of documents containing the word)

Example:
Total documents = 1000
"machine" appears in 10 documents
IDF = log(1000/10) = log(100) ≈ 2


3. TF-IDF Score:
TF-IDF = TF × IDF

Example:
TF-IDF = 0.4 × 2 = 0.8


Interpretation:
- High score → word is important in that document and rare overall
- Low score → word is common across many documents


Note (scikit-learn):
IDF = log((1 + N) / (1 + df)) + 1
# to avoid 0/0 case
